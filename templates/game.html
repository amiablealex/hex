How to Play

Goal — be the first to reach the target score.

Turns — each turn you get 3 tile stacks. Place all 3 on empty hexes, then your opponent goes.

Merging — when you place a tile next to one with the same colour on top, they merge automatically. Only the top colour can merge — colours underneath are blocked until the top is moved away.

Clearing — stack 10 of the same colour and they clear, scoring points for you.

Fertile hexes — hexes with a golden border and 2× mark double the points for any clear on them.

Cascades — if a clear triggers another clear, the second scores 2×, the third 3×, and so on. Combine with fertile hexes for huge points.
